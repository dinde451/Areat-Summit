*Notes

The Gnasher: 50% Chance of Open Wounds: This is a chance of making a monster bleed uncontrollably. They lose health while bleeding.
Skull Splitter: Hit Blinds Target: Decreases radius of awareness similar to the Necromancer Curse: Dim Vision.
Deathspade: Hit Blinds Target: Decreases radius of awareness similar to the Necromancer Curse: Dim Vision.
Axe of Fechmar: Note, this is not the same as Cold Damage.
Goreshovel: 60% Chance of Open Wounds: This is a chance of making a monster bleed uncontrollably. They lose health while bleeding.
Humongous: Requirements 20%: Adds 20% more to item requirements. In this case the requirement of Strength on The Humongous is 84 instead of the normal 70 Strength required on Normal Giant Axes.
